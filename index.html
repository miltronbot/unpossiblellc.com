who we are

    
Small team.
Unreasonable scope.

    

      Unpossible LLC is what happens when a couple of engineers refuse to accept that a thing is too big to build.
      We ship software that reads markets, hardware that mines dollars from electricity, and infrastructure that
      turns two laptops into one relentless machine. The name is a wink — "me build that? that's unpossible" —
      and also a promise.
    

    

      
3
shipping products
A weekly-chart pattern scanner, a container-scale GPU cloud, and a distributed dev mesh — all live, all built lean.

the mission

    

      Build things that are impossible unpossible — useful,
      a little weird, and shipped by a team far too small to be doing this. Make software that respects your
      attention, hardware that pays for itself, and tools we'd actually want to use. Have fun, or it doesn't count.
    

    

      
01
Ship the weird one
The obvious idea is already taken. We chase the version everyone else skipped because it sounded too hard.

      
02
Own the metal
Real hardware, real data, real ownership. No renting our destiny from someone else's dashboard.

      
03
Small on purpose
Fewer people, tighter loops, faster ships. Constraints are the whole point.

      
04
Leave a door unlocked
Every thing we make hides something for the curious. This site included.
